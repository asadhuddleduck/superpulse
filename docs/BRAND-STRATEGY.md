# SuperPulse Brand Strategy

> Prepared for Huddle Duck Ltd | April 2026
> Brand engagement led by discovery interview with founder Asad Shah

---

## 1. Brand Positioning Statement

**For local restaurant owners** who post on Instagram but don't have time or expertise to run ads, **SuperPulse is the AI-powered boosting engine** that automatically turns every post into a local ad — 24/7, with zero effort. **Unlike Boosterberg** and manual boosting through the Instagram app, SuperPulse makes all 9 decisions autonomously while you sleep, saves 30% on ad costs, and delivers results that feel like the future arrived early.

**One-line positioning:** SuperPulse is the silent partner that makes every restaurant post reach the whole neighbourhood.

---

## 2. Brand Essence

### The Core Feeling
**"The future is here, and I got here first."**

SuperPulse exists at the intersection of two emotions:
- **Primary: Wonder** — the feeling of using technology that shouldn't exist yet. Ahmed doesn't understand how it works, but he understands the results. That gap is where the magic lives.
- **Secondary: Power** — the quiet confidence of having a secret weapon. Ahmed knows something his competitors don't. He has leverage they can't see.

Wonder attracts. Power retains.

### The Metaphor
**A local power surge.** Energy and awareness radiating outward from the restaurant into the surrounding neighbourhood. Like a shockwave of visibility, pulsing from the kitchen door to every phone within a mile. The pulse is constant, automatic, and unstoppable.

---

## 3. Brand Personality

SuperPulse is **The Silent Partner** — invisible, powerful, always working, never asking for credit.

### Five Personality Traits

| Trait | What it means | What it doesn't mean |
|---|---|---|
| **Autonomous** | Works without being asked. Makes decisions. Runs 24/7. Ahmed forgets it's there until he checks the numbers. | Not detached or cold. The autonomy is in service of Ahmed's success, not indifference to it. |
| **Precise** | Every decision is calculated. Budget, timing, targeting, radius — nothing is random. The AI is surgical. | Not robotic or sterile. Precision here means "wastes nothing," not "feels nothing." |
| **Understated** | Doesn't shout about itself. No flashy animations. No "AI-POWERED!!!" badges. The premium is in the quiet confidence. | Not invisible or forgettable. Understated is a choice, not an absence. The brand has presence — it just doesn't need to prove it. |
| **Ahead** | Feels like it came from the future. The technology, the interface, the results — everything feels one generation ahead of anything else in the market. | Not experimental or unfinished. "Ahead" means polished future, not rough beta. |
| **Warm** | Speaks simply. Uses Ahmed's language. Explains things like a friend, not a manual. The product is futuristic; the communication is human. | Not casual or jokey. Warmth here is clarity and respect, not memes and emojis. |

### Brand Voice Summary
**Looks like 2030. Speaks like your most successful friend.**

## 4. Tone of Voice

### The Principle
SuperPulse's visual identity is premium and futuristic (Tesla dashboard). Its voice is warm, clear, and jargon-free (your mate explaining it over chai). This duality is the brand's defining characteristic. Never sacrifice one for the other.

### Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **Clear** | Every sentence could be understood by someone who's never used a computer. No jargon. No acronyms. No "leverage" or "optimise." |
| **Confident** | Speaks in declaratives, not hedges. "SuperPulse boosts your posts" not "SuperPulse can help boost your posts." |
| **Respectful** | Ahmed is a business owner, not a "user." He's busy, not dumb. Never condescend. Never over-explain. |
| **Concise** | Says it once, says it well. If it takes a paragraph, it should take a sentence. |

### Do / Don't Examples

| Context | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|
| **Explaining the product** | "SuperPulse boosts your Instagram posts automatically. You do nothing." | "Our AI-powered platform leverages machine learning to optimise your social media advertising spend across multiple parameters." |
| **Highlighting savings** | "You save about 30% because we skip Apple's fees." | "By utilising API-level integration, we bypass intermediary platform transaction costs." |
| **Onboarding** | "Connect your Instagram. That's it. We handle the rest." | "Complete the OAuth2 authentication flow to enable automated campaign management." |
| **Describing AI** | "The AI decides what to boost, how much to spend, and when to stop." | "Our proprietary neural network analyses engagement metrics in real-time to dynamically allocate budget." |
| **Error states** | "Something went wrong. We're looking into it." | "An unexpected error has occurred. Please consult our documentation." |
| **Results** | "423 people visited your profile this week." | "Your KPI dashboard indicates a 23% uplift in profile engagement metrics." |

### Banned Words
Never use in customer-facing copy:
- Leverage, optimise, utilize, synergy, paradigm
- End-to-end, holistic, ecosystem, scalable
- AI-powered (in headlines — the AI is the product, not a badge)
- "Smart" as an adjective for the product
- Any acronym the customer wouldn't use (CPM, ROAS, CTR — unless in a pro dashboard)

### Encouraged Language
- "Boost" (the core action word)
- "Automatically" (the core differentiator)
- "Your posts" (ownership stays with Ahmed)
- "While you sleep" (the aspiration)
- "Every post" / "24/7" / "always on" (consistency)
- "Neighbourhood" / "local" / "nearby" (the radius)

## 5. Visual Identity Direction

### Brand Family: Huddle Duck DNA
SuperPulse is a product of Huddle Duck Ltd and should feel like it. Same colour palette, same dark-first aesthetic, same premium energy. The only difference: **a lightning bolt replaces the duck** — signalling speed, energy, and automated power.

This isn't a separate brand. It's the Huddle Duck product brand for AI-powered boosting. A customer who knows Huddle Duck should immediately recognise the family resemblance.

### Visual Principles

1. **Dark-first.** Black and near-black backgrounds are the canvas. Everything else is light on dark. This isn't a theme option — it's the brand. (Inherited from Huddle Duck.)

2. **Yellow as signal.** Sandstorm yellow (#F7CE46) is the hero colour — the same yellow as Huddle Duck. It's the lightning bolt, the CTAs, the moments of energy. Used sparingly, it pops against the dark canvas.

3. **Green as accent.** Viridian green (#1EBA8F) plays a supporting role — active states, metrics, success indicators. The Huddle Duck secondary colour, carried forward.

4. **The Lightning Bolt.** The bolt is the brand mark. It appears as the icon, the favicon, the motif. It communicates: instant, electric, powerful. Where Huddle Duck has a duck, SuperPulse has a bolt.

5. **Breathing room.** Premium is communicated through space, not through adding more. Generous margins. Deliberate emptiness. The brand breathes.

### Visual References
- Huddle Duck's existing dark UI (the parent brand — direct inheritance)
- Linear's brand system (dark mode, precise, developer-grade but beautiful)
- Stripe's documentation (clarity, whitespace, confidence)
- Flash/bolt iconography from Figma, Bolt, Zap — minimal, geometric, sharp

## 6. Colour Palette

### Philosophy
SuperPulse uses the Huddle Duck colour system directly. Same yellow, same green, same dark canvas. This is a product brand under the Huddle Duck umbrella — visual consistency builds trust and brand recognition.

### Primary Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Base** | Void | `#050508` | 5, 5, 8 | Primary background. The canvas everything lives on. |
| **Surface** | Graphite | `#111116` | 17, 17, 22 | Cards, panels, elevated surfaces. Subtle lift from base. |
| **Border** | Slate | `#1E1E26` | 30, 30, 38 | Dividers, borders, subtle separations. |

### Accent Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Accent** | Sandstorm Yellow | `#F7CE46` | 247, 206, 70 | THE brand colour. The lightning bolt. CTAs, hero elements, conversion moments. Warm, energetic, unmistakable. |
| **Secondary Accent** | Viridian Green | `#1EBA8F` | 30, 186, 143 | Active states, metrics, success indicators, dashboard data. The working colour. |
| **Secondary Muted** | Viridian 60% | `#1EBA8F99` | 30, 186, 143, 0.6 | Hover states, secondary indicators. |

### Text Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Primary Text** | White | `#F0F0F5` | 240, 240, 245 | Headings, key body text. Slightly warm white — never pure #FFFFFF. |
| **Secondary Text** | Mist | `#8888A0` | 136, 136, 160 | Supporting text, labels, timestamps. Readable but receded. |
| **Tertiary Text** | Shadow | `#555566` | 85, 85, 102 | Placeholder text, disabled states. Present but quiet. |

### Colour Rules
1. **Sandstorm yellow for action and identity.** The bolt, CTAs, "Get Started" buttons, key highlights. It's the brand's signature — warm, energetic, unmistakable.
2. **Viridian green for data and status.** Active campaigns, metrics, success states, dashboard elements. The working colour of the product.
3. **Dark backgrounds only.** The brand does not have a "light mode." If a context requires light (e.g., email), use Graphite (#111116) as the background, never white.
4. **Same colours as Huddle Duck.** If you're choosing a colour and wondering whether to match Huddle Duck — yes. Always yes. That's the point.

## 7. Typography

### Philosophy
Typography must serve the duality: premium and futuristic in appearance, effortlessly readable for Ahmed. No novelty. No quirk. Clean, geometric, confident.

### Recommended Stack

| Role | Font | Weight | Fallback | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Display / Headlines** | **Inter** | 700 (Bold), 800 (ExtraBold) | system-ui, -apple-system | Geometric, modern, exceptional legibility at all sizes. Free. Used by Linear, Vercel, Notion. Feels premium without trying. |
| **Body** | **Inter** | 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium) | system-ui, -apple-system | Consistent with headlines. Inter was designed for screens — optimised for the exact context SuperPulse lives in. |
| **Monospace / Data** | **JetBrains Mono** | 400, 500 | 'SF Mono', 'Fira Code', monospace | For metrics, numbers, dashboard data. Technical precision. The "cockpit" feeling. |

### Typography Rules
1. **All caps for labels only.** "PROFILE VISITS" or "THIS WEEK" — never for sentences or headings. All caps in body text reads as shouting.
2. **Large numbers, small labels.** Metrics should be oversized (32-48px) with small grey labels (12-14px). The number is the hero.
3. **Generous line height.** 1.5-1.6 for body text. The brand breathes — so does the type.
4. **Letter-spacing on headings.** Slight negative tracking (-0.02em) on bold headlines gives density and premium feel.

### Alternative Consideration
If a more distinctive display face is desired for marketing (not in-product):
- **Satoshi** — geometric sans with slightly more personality than Inter. Good for headlines on the website.
- **General Sans** — similar to Satoshi, slightly warmer. Good bridge between premium and approachable.

## 8. Logo Direction Brief

### Concept: The Lightning Bolt

The logo mark is a **lightning bolt in sandstorm yellow (#F7CE46)** — the SuperPulse equivalent of Huddle Duck's duck. Simple, geometric, sharp. It communicates: instant power, energy, speed, automation.

### Direction

**The mark** is a minimal, geometric lightning bolt:
- Sharp angles, clean lines — not cartoonish, not overly detailed
- A single-stroke style bolt with angular zig-zag shape
- Flat colour, no gradients, no 3D effects
- Must read clearly at 16x16 as a favicon

**The wordmark** "SuperPulse" sits beside or below the mark:
- Inter Bold or a custom geometric sans
- White (#F0F0F5) on dark backgrounds
- Clean, no effects, no outlines

### Mark Principles
1. **Works at all sizes.** From 1024x1024 app icon down to 16x16 favicon. The bolt shape is inherently scalable.
2. **Works in mono.** Must be legible in pure white on black AND pure black on white.
3. **No literal imagery.** No forks, no plates, no food. No Instagram logo. No Meta anything. The bolt is industry-agnostic.
4. **No text in the mark.** The bolt stands alone without any letters or words.
5. **Family resemblance.** Same visual weight and simplicity as the Huddle Duck duck mark. A customer familiar with Huddle Duck should immediately feel the connection.

### App Icon Specification
- 1024x1024 for Meta/Apple submission
- Lightning bolt centred on a Void (#050508) background
- Sandstorm Yellow (#F7CE46) for the bolt
- No text, no wordmark — bolt only
- Generous padding (the bolt should occupy ~50-60% of the icon area)
- No glow or effects — flat and clean, matching the Huddle Duck icon style

## 10. Brand Architecture

### Naming Convention
- **Product:** SuperPulse (always one word, capital S capital P)
- **Parent company:** Huddle Duck Ltd — visible in footer, about page, and legal. Not hidden — the parent brand adds credibility.
- **Relationship language:** "SuperPulse by Huddle Duck" or "A Huddle Duck product"
- **Never:** Super Pulse, superpulse, SUPERPULSE, SP
- **Domain:** superpulse.io

### Tagline Candidates
In order of recommendation:
1. **"Every post. Every person. Automatically."** — Clear, rhythmic, says exactly what it does
2. **"Your posts, boosted while you sleep."** — Warm, benefit-led, speaks to Ahmed directly
3. **"The AI that boosts your restaurant."** — Simple, direct, category-defining
4. **"Set it. Forget it. Grow."** — Action-oriented, emphasises the zero-effort promise

### Sub-brand / Feature Naming
Future features should follow the energy/signal metaphor:
- Pulse Radius (targeting area)
- Pulse Budget (spend management)
- Pulse Score (post performance metric)
- Signal (the analytics/reporting section)
